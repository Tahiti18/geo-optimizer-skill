1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the website geoready.dev (the “Site”), owned by Tahiti18.

Respecting your privacy is our top priority. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union and all applicable privacy regulations.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: GitHub issues

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data strictly necessary for the operation of the Site and the provision of our services.

2.1 Automatic browsing data

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect some browsing information:

  • Technical cookies: to maintain the session and your preferences
  • IP address: (for security purposes only, not for tracking)
  • Device information: browser type, operating system
  • Log data: pages visited, access time, visit duration

2.2 User-provided data

When you use our services, the following data may be collected:

  • URLs to audit: to provide the SEO analysis service
  • Comments and feedback: submitted via contact form
  • Contact details: if you reach out to us, only if voluntarily provided

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use technical and analytics cookies to improve your browsing experience.

Essential cookies (required): These cookies are essential for the operation of the Site and cannot be disabled. They include cookies for authentication, security, and session preferences.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics to analyze Site usage. Data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not perform any personal identification. You can disable these cookies via the cookie settings button in the bottom-left corner.

Marketing cookies (Google Ads): Used only with your explicit consent to show personalized ads. Can be disabled at any time.

How to manage cookies:

You can accept, reject, or manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings button in the bottom-left corner of the page.

4. Google Analytics

The Site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC (“Google”). Google uses the collected cookies to analyze user behavior on the Site.

What we collect: - Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referral source
- Device type and browser
- Geographic location (country/region level only, not full IP address)

What we do NOT collect: - Full IP addresses (they are anonymized)
- Personally identifiable information
- Financial or banking information
- Sensitive data (health, religion, politics, etc.)

Anonymization: Google Analytics is configured to anonymize users' IP addresses before transmission to Google. Data is retained for 24 months.

Opt-out: You can disable Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out add-on for your browser.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against:

  • Unauthorized access: protection with two-factor authentication
  • Disclosure or destruction: TLS 1.3 encryption for all connections
  • Alteration: data integrity verification via checksums
  • Loss: regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

Data is stored on Render.com and GitHub Pages servers, both GDPR-compliant with infrastructure in the European Union.

6. Data Retention

Data type Retention period Legal basis
Browsing data 6 months Legitimate interest (Art. 6 GDPR)
Analytics cookies 24 months Consent (Art. 9 GDPR)
User feedback 24 months Legitimate interest
Audited URLs 24 months (anonymized) Legitimate interest

7. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We share data only in the following cases:

Recipient Purpose Country
Google LLC Google Analytics (traffic analysis) EU (data centers)
GitHub Inc. Site hosting (GitHub Pages) EU (France)
Render.com Backend hosting (API) EU (Germany)

All the above parties are registered sub-processors and guarantee levels of data protection compliant with the GDPR.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: obtain confirmation that your data exists and request access to it
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction: restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data on legitimate grounds
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw consent at any time (without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing)
  • Lodge a complaint: submit a complaint to the supervisory authority (the Italian Data Protection Authority — Garante per la protezione dei dati personali)

To exercise your rights, write to: GitHub issues

We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request. We may ask you to verify your identity for security reasons.

9. Minors

The Site is not directed at persons under the age of 16. We do not intentionally collect personal data from minors. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it within 30 days.

10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect upon publication on this page.

We will notify you by email (if you have provided an address) or via a notice on the Site in case of significant changes.

We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we protect your data.
